About Me

I absolutely love to code! I've just finished the Full-Stack Web Development program with Lambda School and am seeking Endorsement with them. They teach skills that are in demand, both technology and soft skills. I've learned so much from my time here! I've completed the Core curriculum, Labs, and Computer Science. The Core curriculum taught HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React with Redux, and Node.js. Labs was an 8 week experience set to prepare us for beginning your career. We worked closely with a team to create a professional product. Some projects started from scratch, and some had been worked on by previous Labs teams. Ours was one that had previous teams, and will have more teams after us. In the Computer Science portion, I learned Python3 and algorithms.

I look forward to meeting with people and discussing your amazing opportunities! You can check out my skills and profile sections below.
