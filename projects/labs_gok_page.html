What is this?

The Garden of Knowledge is a student management system for an extracurricular English language program, currently in Bahrain with scalability to expand to other areas. Several previous teams have worked on this project, as will other teams after us to continue building towards our stakeholder's vision.

What kind of build was this?

Labs consists of a project we completed on a remote, cross-functional team of 5 over 8 weeks. I worked as a Full-Stack Developer, as well as conducted user research. The product was/is being built according to our stakeholder's needs and vision.

What did I do?

  • I assisted with the planning of each release canvas, and identifying key results for each product cycle.
  • I created and administered user surveys to capture accurate numbers before and after each product cycle for the key results.
  • I updated the PVD as needed with notes.
  • I implemented student cards with routing and edit option for the user dashboard.
  • I implemented functionality to add a new student to a family from the admin dashboard, and see all other students in a family.
  • I created placement test functionality on the adimin dashboard.
  • I fixed the database migration for the placement_test table, among others.
  • I created a model, controller, and endpoints for the placement_test.
  • I assisted teammates with other tasks as needed.
